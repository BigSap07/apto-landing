Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

What we collect

When you sign up for the waitlist, we collect your email address. When you create an account, we collect your name, email, and any data you enter into the platform (training logs, nutrition data, check-ins, progress photos, messages).

How we use it

Your data is used solely to provide the Apto coaching platform. We don't sell your data. We don't share it with advertisers. Your coach sees only the data you submit through the platform.

Where it's stored

All data is stored on Supabase servers in Frankfurt, Germany (EU). Files and images are stored in Supabase Storage, also EU-hosted.

Your rights (GDPR)

You can request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data at any time by emailing hello@getapto.com. You can delete your account and all associated data from within the app.

Cookies

We use essential cookies for authentication only. No tracking cookies. No analytics cookies.

Contact

Questions? Email hello@getapto.com.
